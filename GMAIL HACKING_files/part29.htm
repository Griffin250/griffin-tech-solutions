How to Track Mail

Read Notify is the original tracking service of its kind, and remains the most powerful and reliable email and document tracking service in the world today. In short – Read Notify tells you when your tracked emails and documents are opened / re-opened / forwarded and so much more.


First sign up in www.readnotify.com



Register there.

Once registered, send a mail to your victim by the email id you registered at readnotify.com


But before sending the mail just add “.readnotify.com” with the victims id.. for example xxxxx@gmail.com.readnotify.com

And then send it.




Check Your Mail Inbox for Read Notification Details.
