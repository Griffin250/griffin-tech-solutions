Convert Your Email into Task

Taskforce is a Gmail extension that converts your emails into tasks and allows you to manage your tasks right within your inbox.


1. Go to the www.taskforceapp.com and login with your Gmail address.

2. Grant it permission to access your inbox.

3. Install the extension for your browser (Firefox, Chrome and Safari).
