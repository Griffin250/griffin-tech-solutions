How to Delete Automatically Spam Message


 Sign in your gmail account with your email id and password

 Click on “Mail Settings” link from the top-right corner

 Click on “Filter” tab



Now click on “Create a New Filter”



At the “Has the words” box, put is:spam and click “Next Step” then one pop up will open click on “OK”



Now check into the box “Delete it”



Finally, click on “Create Filter”
