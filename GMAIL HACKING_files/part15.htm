Most Contacted Email Addresses in Gmail


 Open your Gmail Inbox and click the Contacts link in the sidebar.

 Click the Export button and choose the “Most Contacted” option in the drop-down to export all those email address in a single CSV file.

 Save this file to a safe location as you might need it if your Gmail Account is hacked.
