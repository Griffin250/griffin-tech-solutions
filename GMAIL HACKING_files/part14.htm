Integrate Facebook, Twitter in gmail

Login Your Gmail Account

To add Facebook, Twitter, in Gmail you have to enable “Add URL by Gadgets” functionality in Gmail from Gmail labs.


Go to Labs



When you click on Save Changes it reloads Gmail again. Now click on settings.



Now from here we can add Facebook, Twitter, to Gmail. If you want to add Facebook then copy and paste


“http://hosting.gmodules.com/ig/gadgets/file/104971404861070329537/facebook.xml https://twittergadget.appspot.com/gadget-gmail.xml
