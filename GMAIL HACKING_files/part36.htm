Send Mail in Your Mobile


 First Sign up an account with way2sms.com

 After Signup you will get a confirmation code on the Mobile to prove your Authentication, if you verify the code your account will be activated.

 Login to your account in way2sms.com

 After that Go to Mail Alerts Section, which is just located besides, see the bellow image.



There is an option called as Activate the Mail Alerts. Just click on Activate button to Activate the Mail alerts on your Mobile phone.



 Login to your Gmail account

 Click on settings & then click on ‘FORWARDING/POP/IMAP‘option

 Select ‘Forward a copy of incoming mail to” option & Enter your Way2SMS Email

ID10291437@way2sms.com there.



 Gmail sends a verification code to 10291437@way2sms.com.

 You will receive gmail confirmation mail alert on your mobile with subject line containing confirmation code.

 Copy and paste the verification code in Gmail verification box. Click On Verify.

 Finished. You will now receive alerts for every incoming mail that arrives in your gmail id.
